SERVICES

Our Services

eModal is the technology fabric that connects Motor Carriers, Shippers and Logistic Service Providers with Terminal Operators & Port Authorities.

Port Manager

eModal Community Portal [eCP] may be deployed in a custom branded manner as found in the Ports of NY/NJ [PortTruckPass / TIPS], Virginia [ProPASS], South Carolina, Oakland and the Portal of Panama

Intermodal Manager

eModal Community Portal [eCP] also connects rail terminal and inland depot stakeholders as deployed in a custom branded manner at the Genesee & Wyoming United Kingdom [Freightliner / Pentalver] operations

Appointments

Timed appointments, Pre-advice notifications configured to optimize the terminal yard from the inside out to speed & automate gate operations prior to truck arrival.

Cargo Visibility

eCP Container Watchlist of Import availability, Export booking claims management, Vessel return instructions, Container lifecycle & Event status, Automated subscriptions and freight notifications across over 75 terminals

Fee Collections

Online fee collections & order to case management [Port, Terminal, Gate, Line, Drayage order, Driver fee settlement & Chassis Rental]. as well as Ocean Carrier North American fee collections.

eModal Data Services

Real-time data integrated directly via API into your TMS or truck dispatch system for cargo visibility, container status, book appointments or pay terminal or line fees. click Here for more info.
